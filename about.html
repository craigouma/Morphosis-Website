About Us

MORPHOSIS LTD

MORPHOSIS is a multi-disciplinary architectural and design firm with a focus on the development of quality, authentic, contemporary African “Creative Spaces”.

It is our desire that vitality and beauty of the built environment plays a crucial role in the creation of a productive and healthy community.

It is for this reason that MORPHOSIS’ philosophy is driven by the “E-volution”.

"E-volution"
ETHICS

Honesty in design, Response to site, Commitment to client, Financial Integrity





ENERGY

Energy efficient buildings through use of natural light and ventilation, specification of energy efficient fittings.



ENVIRONMENT

Green buildings. They must have a positive impact on the environment on which they sit. Trees. Waste water management and recycling. Rain water harvesting.

ERGONOMICS

The human being must always be at the center of all our buildings. All measurements, specifications, sizes, effects, materials, lighting levels must respect the human scale and comfort.

ECONOMY

Less is More. BIG ideas, less Materiality, Less walls, MORE space, Less Cost, More returns.






EXPERIENCE

Target all the senses, in all seasons, at all times. Enrich and Enhance life.






EMOTIONS

Elicit lasting emotional attachment to resident and guest.






ESTHETICS

Our buildings must be beautiful but this beauty must be based on a high philosophical base.






ENOVATION

Learning is at the heart of creativity and each project must try out something new to add to this volution.
